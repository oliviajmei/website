design timeline

a breakdown of key subsystem designs developed for uc '26.

rack and pinion housing

custom steering housing designed around manufacturability and subsystem packaging. the housing was manually milled from aluminum and integrated with a hand-lathed shaft and 3d printed bushings to support the pinion assembly and rack travel.

autonomous braking system

compact braking actuation package integrating a gearbox, bearings, electromagnetic clutch, and 3d printed motor mount. the assembly was designed to deliver controlled braking through a toleranced packaged system with serviceable components.
